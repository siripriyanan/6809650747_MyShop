About us

BLACK is a simple color - but it has such a powerful identity that represents confidence, elegance,

mystery, and versatility. In the 16th century, it was perceived as a symbol of power and wealth

and black in fashion became to be very luxurious and ageless for centuries. Black allows us to

focus on the elements of the garments or the look in the absence of color on our clothes. Furthermore,

we use the black logo that is monochromatic to convey a sense of sophistication and exclusivity.
